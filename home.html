Common Animal Diseases

Canine Parvovirus

Highly contagious viral disease. Symptoms: vomiting, diarrhea.

Treatment: Supportive care and hydration therapy.

Foot and Mouth Disease

Severe viral disease in livestock. Symptoms: blisters, fever.

Treatment: Vaccination and quarantine measures.

Feline Leukemia Virus

Contagious virus affecting cats. Symptoms: anemia, immune suppression.

Treatment: Supportive treatments.
